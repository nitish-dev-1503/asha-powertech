# Mobile Lighting Towers

## Overview
Our Mobile Lighting Towers provide efficient, portable illumination for construction sites, events, and emergency response operations.

![Mobile Lighting Towers](/MLT.png)

## Key Features

- **Portability**: Easy to transport and set up
- **High Lumen Output**: Up to 8000 lumens per tower
- **Fuel & Electric Options**: Choose based on your needs
- **Extended Runtime**: Up to 12 hours on single tank
- **Durable Construction**: Heavy-duty steel frame

## Specifications

### Lighting System
- LED technology (3000K-5000K color temperature)
- Adjustable beam angle (15° to 60°)
- Flicker-free operation
- IP65 water resistance rating

### Power Options
- **Diesel**: 2.5L tank, 12-hour runtime
- **Electric**: 220V connection available
- **Hybrid**: Battery backup option

### Height & Coverage
- Telescopic mast: 3m to 5m adjustable height
- Coverage area: 200-500 sqm depending on height
- 360° rotatable light head

## Applications

- **Construction Sites**: Night shift work illumination
- **Events**: Outdoor concerts, festivals, sports
- **Emergency Response**: Disaster sites, accident scenes
- **Security**: Perimeter lighting, surveillance areas
- **Mining**: Underground and open-pit operations

## Setup & Operation

- **Setup Time**: Less than 10 minutes
- **Weight**: 120-150 kg (easy to transport)
- **Transportation**: Standard trailer or pickup truck
- **Operation**: Single person operation possible

## Safety Features

- Automatic shutoff mechanism
- Overload protection
- Temperature monitoring
- UV stability rating
- Anti-tip design

## Maintenance

- Monthly: Clean lens and reflectors
- Quarterly: Check fuel/battery levels
- Annually: Professional inspection and service
