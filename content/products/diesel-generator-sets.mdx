# Diesel Generator Sets [10 kVA to 20 kVA]

## Overview

Sharda Diesels offers reliable diesel generator sets ranging from **10 kVA to 20 kVA** designed for uninterrupted backup power. These gensets are ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial use where continuous power supply is critical.

![Diesel Generator Sets Banner](/DGS.png)

## Key Features

### Compact Design
- Quick switchover to backup power  
- Excellent recovery time  
- Stable voltage regulation protects connected equipment  

### Large Fuel Tank
- Powered by compact, low-vibration Mahindra engines  
- Suitable for rooftop installations  

### Longer Engine Life
- CPCB compliant for noise and emission norms  

### Low Maintenance
- Fuel-efficient zero-leak engines  
- Manual and AMF control panel options  
- Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) available  
- Authorized installation and servicing  

### Industry Applications
- Telecom  
- Defence  
- Railways  
- Petrol pumps  
- Hospitals  
- Supermarkets  
- Banks  
- Construction sites
